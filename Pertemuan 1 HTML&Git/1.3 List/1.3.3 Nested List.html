Steps on how to make Instant Noodles

  • Prepare some instant noodles (preferably Indomie)
  • Cook some water until its boiling(around 300ml)
    • If preferred you can cook some other ingredients to add into the noodles
  • Stirs the noodles until they separate each other
    • Its optional as well if you want the noodles to be half-cooked so you can get that hard chunky bite
  • While waiting until the noodles are cooked, prepare the seasonings on a plate/bowl
  • Once the noodles are separated and isn't sticky/hard anymore, drain the water from the noodles
  • Put the noodles into a plate/bowl, and mix the seasonings evenly
  • Congratulations ! You just made an instant noodles !
