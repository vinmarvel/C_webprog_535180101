Steps on how to make Instant Noodles

  1. Prepare some instant noodles (preferably Indomie)
  2. Cook some water until its boiling(around 300ml)
  3. Stirs the noodles until they separate each other
  4. While waiting until the noodles are cooked, prepare the seasonings on a plate/bowl
  5. Once the noodles are separated and isn't sticky/hard anymore, drain the water from the noodles
  6. Put the noodles into a plate/bowl, and mix the seasonings evenly
  7. Congratulations ! You just made an instant noodles !
